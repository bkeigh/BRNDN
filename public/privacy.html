Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

BRNDN Sports Tracker ("BRNDN", "we", "us") provides live sports scores, statistics, and betting odds for news and entertainment. This policy explains what limited data we handle when you use the site. BRNDN is intended for adults aged 21 or older.

Information we collect

  • No account required. We do not ask you to register, and we do not collect your name, email, or payment details to use the tracker.
  • Local device storage. We store small preferences in your browser's localStorage — for example, your confirmation that you are 21+ and your last-used settings. This never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us.
  • Usage analytics. We may use privacy-friendly, aggregate analytics to understand traffic (pages viewed, sport popularity, referring site). Where required, this runs only after you consent.
  • Advertising & affiliate partners. We may display ads or link to licensed sportsbooks. Those third parties may set their own cookies or use device identifiers subject to their own privacy policies.

Cookies & consent

Essential storage (like your age confirmation) is always active. Any analytics or advertising cookies are governed by a consent banner where required by law (e.g. GDPR / CPRA). You can change or withdraw consent at any time through that banner, and you can clear all stored data by clearing your browser site data.

Third-party data sources

Scores, schedules, standings, and odds are sourced from third-party sports data feeds and are provided for informational purposes only. We do not guarantee their accuracy, completeness, or timeliness. Logos, team names, and league marks are the property of their respective owners.

Sportsbook affiliate links

BRNDN may earn a commission when you click a sportsbook link and open or fund an account. This does not change the odds you see or cost you anything. Offers are shown only where the operator is licensed, and may be restricted by your location.

Age requirement

BRNDN is not directed to anyone under 21. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided information, contact us and we will remove it.

Your choices

  • Decline non-essential cookies via the consent banner.
  • Clear localStorage / site data to remove the age confirmation and preferences.
  • Use your browser's "Do Not Track" / Global Privacy Control signals where supported.
