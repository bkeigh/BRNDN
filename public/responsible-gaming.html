Responsible Gaming

BRNDN is intended for adults 21 and older.

BRNDN Sports Tracker shows scores, stats, and betting odds for entertainment only — we do not accept wagers. If you choose to bet through a licensed sportsbook, please keep it fun and within your means. Betting should never be a way to make money or escape problems.

Get help now

1-800-GAMBLER24/7 confidential national helpline ncpgambling.orgNational Council on Problem Gambling Text "HELP" to 53342Confidential text support

Warning signs

  • Betting more than you can afford to lose, or chasing losses.
  • Spending more time or money on gambling than intended.
  • Borrowing money or neglecting work, school, or relationships to bet.
  • Feeling restless, anxious, or irritable when trying to cut back.

Stay in control

  • Set a budget and a time limit before you start — and stick to them.
  • Never bet with money meant for essentials.
  • Don't gamble to win back losses.
  • Use sportsbook tools: deposit limits, time-outs, and self-exclusion.
  • Take regular breaks; treat any winnings as a bonus, not income.

Self-exclusion

Most licensed sportsbooks and U.S. states offer self-exclusion programs that block your accounts for a set period. Contact your sportsbook or state gaming regulator to enroll.
